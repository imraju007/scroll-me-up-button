== Description ==
<p>Scroll Me Up Button is a lightweight and customizable WordPress plugin that adds a scroll to top button on your site. With 19+ switch icons to choose from, you can easily customize the look of the button to match your site's design. Additionally, you can customize the position of the button on your page, choosing from several placement options.</p>

<h3>FEATURES</h3>
<ol>
	<li>One-click enable/disable</li>
	<li>19+ switch icons to choose from</li>
    <li>Customizable button text</li>
    <li>Customizable button background color</li>
    <li>Customizable button position on page</li>
    <li>Options to show/hide button on mobile devices</li>
    <li>Option to enable/disable smooth scroll animation</li>
    <li>Option to add custom CSS for further customization</li>
</ol>



<h3>HOW IT WORKS</h3>
<p>The plugin is very easy to use in WordPress. Just follow these quick steps-</p>
<ol>
	<li>Install Scroll Me Up Button and <strong>activate</strong> it.</li>
	<li>Check your website, and you should already see the scroll to top floating switch at the bottom right.</li>
	<li>To customize, click on the <strong>ScrollMeUp</strong> menu on your WordPress dashboard.</li>
</ol>
